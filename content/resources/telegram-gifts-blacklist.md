# Telegram Gifts Blacklist (Community Reported)

This page maintains a **community-reported blacklist of Telegram Gifts** that are believed to be stolen or compromised following account takeovers or unauthorized access.

The goal is **risk awareness**, not accusation.

Telegram currently offers limited recovery mechanisms for compromised accounts and gifted assets. As a result, stolen gifts may reappear in secondary markets without clear provenance. This blacklist exists to help collectors, traders, and platforms identify **reported-risk items** before interacting with them.

---

## How this blacklist works

- All entries are **community-reported**
- Every item is listed with status **Reported**
- Reports are reviewed, logged, and archived internally
- Reporter identities are **not public**
- No assumptions of guilt or ownership are made

If a gift is recovered or a report is proven inaccurate, the entry may be updated or removed after review.

---

## Disclaimer

This blacklist contains **community-submitted reports** intended solely for informational and risk-awareness purposes.

Entries do **not** constitute legal determinations, ownership claims, or accusations.  
If you believe an entry is inaccurate or can provide proof of recovery or legitimate ownership, contact Chieflabz for review.
